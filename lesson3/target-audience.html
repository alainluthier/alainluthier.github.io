Target Audience

The target audience of the digitalweather.org web site is described bellow:

  • Who: Meteorologists, journalists, and others interested in weather news and information.
  • Age: 14 to 100
  • Motivation: Staying informed regarding news related to the climate and carrying out analysis tasks using the technology.
